Currently

Generative AI for enterprise wireless networks.

At Extreme Networks I lead the data-science work that combines large language model reasoning with classical statistical signal processing and domain knowledge graphs. The systems I build investigate wireless-client behaviour, roaming issues and device-performance anomalies across thousands of access points and switches. They have reduced investigation time on common networking problems from hours to seconds.

The work is underpinned by a research career in probabilistic graphical models, multi-objective decision making under uncertainty and preference elicitation, conducted in collaboration with IBM Research Labs Dublin and United Technology Research Centre, and published in the leading venues for artificial intelligence research.
