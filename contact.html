A short note

On reaching out.

I read messages on LinkedIn and ResearchGate carefully and try to respond to substantive enquiries promptly. The most useful messages are the ones that tell me who you are, what you are working on, and what you would like to talk about, in two or three sentences. I am open to research collaborations, advisory conversations on probabilistic and generative AI for industrial systems, and discussions with teams whose work overlaps with the practice areas described elsewhere on this site.
